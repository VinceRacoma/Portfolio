Samgyeop Grill Box Website

Samgyeop Grill Box, specializes in authentic Korean samgyeopsal and aims to enhance customer experience through a centralized website. Saramify

Saramify is an app designed to help users learn Korean, focusing on vocabulary and grammar for personal growth. Derived from "saram" (Korean for "person"), the app emphasizes individual language development. A team of key participants, guided by the client, collaborates to ensure the app aligns with its vision and delivers an effective learning experience.
